WHERE WILL YOU FIND YOUR NERY?

Bye-Nary is a game where Juan (1) and Nary (0) are living happily together in a memory location inside your processor. The evil forces of memory leaks kidnapped Juan's beloved Nary, and now Juan is on a quest to rescue his Two's Complelment.
